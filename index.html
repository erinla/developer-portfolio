About Me

Once upon a time, many moons ago, I found myself deep in the world of advertising, but something was missing. I was itching for a new opportunity. So, I took a leap of faith, waved goodbye to my comfort zone, and enrolled in an intensive three-month web development bootcamp. I haven't looked back since!

Fast forward five years, and here I am, a seasoned email developer! I have spent this time focused on building, maintaining, and optimizing automated marketing campaigns within Salesforce Marketing Cloud. My expertise also includes developing responsive emails, including transactional and marketing communications.

I've opened doors for myself that I didn't even know existed back in my broadcast days, and I couldn't be more grateful for the journey. I'm also grateful to you for taking the time to read through my portfolio!
